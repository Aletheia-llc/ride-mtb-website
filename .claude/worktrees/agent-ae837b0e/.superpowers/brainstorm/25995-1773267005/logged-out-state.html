What do logged-out visitors see?

Same 3-column layout — but the center feed and left sidebar need a different approach without user history.

A

Popular feed + soft sign-up nudge

Show the same feed layout but with trending/popular content instead of personalized. A small banner at the top says "Sign up to personalize your feed." No hard gate — visitors can browse freely. Same sidebar, but XP widget replaced with a "Join Ride MTB" card.

Best for: SEO, casual discovery, low friction

B

Marketing hero above the feed

Full-width hero section at the top ("The MTB platform built for riders — Learn, Ride, Connect") with a Join CTA. Below it, the same 3-column layout with popular content. The hero collapses once logged in.

Best for: conversion, first impressions, brand impact

C

Interest picker before the feed

Logged-out visitors see a quick "What do you ride?" selector (Trail / Enduro / DH / XC / Beginner) before the feed loads. Their selection personalizes the feed for that session — no account needed. Prompts to save preferences by signing up.

Best for: personalization-first experience, higher sign-up intent
